What is "Happy Server"?

Happy Server is a group of friends that composes of 6 people, Alfonso Servidad, Joaqui Isip, Eugene Limguangco, Ricardo Moreno III, Aiden Mallillin, and Frederick Laid. This friend group was created on August 12, 2020 due to Alfonso messaging Joaqui about an interaction they had on December 4th, 2016 in a birthday party. Because of this, Joaqui and Alfonso started to hang out and eventually, started hanging out with Eugene. Then, Ricardo, Aiden, and Fred got added in the mix throughout the year.


• Alfonso "alfonso312" Servidad

Alfonso "alfonso312" Joaquin F. Servidad is one of the 2 main founders of Happy Server. In early 2020, he quit karate to play with Joaqui and Eugene. He played a variety of video games from Minecraft, to Roblox, to Valorant and many more, until in late 2022, he had to get glasses cause of his weak eyesight. Recently, as of 2023, he started playing sports again more than video games.


• Joaqui "JustHamil" Isip

Joaqui "JustHamil" Gerard M. Isip is the 2nd main founder of Happy Server. Alfonso and Joaqui were already good friends due to them being busmates and the love they share for the "Rubik's Cube". His original name during the early start of Happy Server was "Hamiltreasurer" until he changed it to "JustHamil" in early 2021 due to a suggestion of Alfonso. Joaqui has mainly been the "brains" of the group, especially in Minecraft Speedrunning. He loves to play Minecraft(mostly speedrunning), Geometry Dash, and many more games.
